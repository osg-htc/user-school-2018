Hotel Information¶

We reserved rooms at three area hotels for participants from outside Madison.

  • DoubleTree by Hilton Hotel Madison, 525 West Johnson Street, +1 (608) 251-5511 — free shuttle service available
  • Wisconsin Union Hotel, 1308 West Dayton Street, +1 (608) 263-2600 — next to classroom building
  • HotelRED, 1501 Monroe Street, +1 (608) 819-8228 — close to classroom building, free shuttle service available

Other important hotel information:

  • We pick the hotel for each person
  • You should have received an email with your hotel and confirmation number
  • We pay only for basic room costs — you must provide a credit card to cover extra costs
  • There is one School participant per room; to have friends or family stay with you, please tell us now
